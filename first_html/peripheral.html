Lighting

NiceVeedi LED Studio Lights

These dimmable LED lights offer adjustable levels from 2800-6500K allowing for warm or cold light. This kit comes as a 2 pack including the light, a tripod, and a phone holder. These lights are powered using USB or USB TypeC power sources. There is a more expensive version that only has a wall plug.


Price: $51.99

BANSINE LED Phone Light

This rechargeable light will clip onto your phone allowing for easy portable lighting. This is a great tool for beginners who may need a bit more light for whatever they hope to capture. While the primary use for this was phones, it can also be attached to laptops or cameras for more lighting wherever you go. It's small size makes it very easy to throw in your bag and go.

Price: $14.39


Camera Mounts

SYNCWIRE Tripod Phone Mount

This mount will allow you to attach your phone to anything with a 1/4" screw. This means any standard tripod or mount could be used with your mobile device. This is great for beginners who want to level up their content but don't want to spend hundreds on a good camera.


Price: $9.99

Amazon Basics Tripod

This 50-inch adjustable tripod is lightweight and allows for multiple different heights. At its smallest this tripod could be 16" and at its tallest this tripod is 50". It also comes with a carrying bag for easier portability.


Price: $17.09

LINKCOOL Octopus Tripod

This tripod has malleable legs allowing you to set up your camera wherever you go. This tripod comes with a phone mount allowing novice creators to take advantage of its capabilities. It's also compatible with your average camera or GoPro. This product is great for filming on the go or in awkward locations.


Price: $18.99

Miscellaneous

HUION Inspiroy H640P

The HUION H640P is a 6"x4" drawing tablet which will allow you to create digital art as if you were drawing with a pen. It measures Pen Pressure meaning it can tell how hard you press down allowing for a more realistic experience.


Price: $39.99

Marengo Micarm

A micarm is good for holding and moving a microphone. Its helpful for getting your mic off your desk and away from a loud keyboard. It also allows you to move your mic when recording things like instruments which may be at different heights or locations.


Price: $19.99

M-WAVE 25 MIDI Keyboard

This keyboard will allow you to more easily make music. When using a DAW you can use this to play notes on different instruments. There's also 8 drum pads which allow users to easily create a beat. There's also 8 knobs allowing you to easily customize your sound.


Price: $89.99
